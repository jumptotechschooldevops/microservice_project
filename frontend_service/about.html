Our Story

From a Single Blog Post to a Global Academy

LinuxPro Academy started in 2018 when our founder, Maria Chen, posted a series of Linux tutorials that unexpectedly went viral. Thousands of students emailed asking for a structured course — so she built one.

What began as a weekend project grew into a full-scale training platform with 6 courses, 4 expert instructors, and a thriving community of 12,000+ learners across 87 countries. We've helped students land roles at Google, Amazon, Red Hat, and hundreds of startups.

Today, LinuxPro Academy is recognized as one of the best independent Linux training providers — not because we're the biggest, but because we genuinely care about every student's success.
